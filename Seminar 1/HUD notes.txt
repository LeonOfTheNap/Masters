Notes for HUD (Heads-Up Display):

1. Consistency with theme and style - shape, colors, contrast, scale
2. Claritiy and readibility - absorb a lot of information at a glance; take into consideration color palette and background (clearly seen)
3. Smart placement of HUD elements - top left for fastest "readbility"
4. "Need to know" basis - too much HUD = immersion-breaking; e.g. consider blod spats when taking damage
5. Customizable where you can
